About Us

We are a team of researchers and developers working on improving coastal fishing practices using data-driven tools.

Our Story

The Coastal Fish Market Platform began in 2023 when a team of marine researchers and tech developers recognized the challenges faced by local fishing communities. With declining fish stocks and unpredictable market prices, fishermen were struggling to make sustainable decisions about when and where to fish.

We set out to create a platform that would provide real-time data and predictive insights to help fishermen maximize their catch while maintaining sustainable practices. Today, our platform serves over 500 registered fishermen across multiple coastal communities.

Our Mission

To empower fishing communities with data-driven insights for sustainable practices and improved livelihoods.

Our Vision

Creating a connected coastal ecosystem where fishers, markets, and consumers benefit from transparency and sustainability.
